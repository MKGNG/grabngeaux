In The Community

More than a corner store.

Grab -N- Geaux is family-owned and Louisiana-rooted. From Little League sponsorships to hurricane relief, we show up for the towns that show up for us. Here's a look at how we get involved.

Youth Sports

We sponsor Little League, softball, and school teams across Southwest Louisiana — jerseys, gear, and end-of-season cookouts on us.

Storm Relief

When the weather turns, our stores stay open as long as it's safe — fuel, water, ice, and hot food for first responders and neighbors alike.

Local Fundraisers

Round-up-at-the-register drives and in-store fundraisers for local schools, churches, and families in need — every dollar stays in the community.

Hosting an event or fundraiser? We'd love to help.
